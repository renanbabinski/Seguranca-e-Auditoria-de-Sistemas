the new york stock exchange is the biggest and most active stock exchange in the world. over half of all adult americans have some, if not all, of their savings invested directly on wall street, so it is not surprising that the fluctuations of the streets famous indexes, the dow jones and the nasdaq, are followed daily by millions of ordinary americans. when the dow and the nasdaq are on a rise, millions of americans feel more prosperous when they are falling, millions start feeling worried about their financial security and their retirement years. yet more importantly, when wall street booms it is a sign that the american economy is booming, creating jobs and prosperity for people throughout the nation when wall street slumps for more than a short period, it is because the american economy is slowing down, putting investment and jobs at risk.
